Spaghetti and Meat(less)balls

Description:

A healthy take on a delicious classic. This vegan recipe is sure to fill your belly and tantalize your taste buds. Mamma Mia!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pack of whole wheat spaghetti
  • 1 stalk celery
  • 1 medium-sized brown onion
  • 1 grated carrot
  • 1 diced capsicum
  • 100g portabella mushrooms
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 1 bag Gardein (or similar) vegan meatballs
  • 1 block vegan parmesan

Steps:

  1. Bring water to boil in large pot (using a touch of olive oil and salt to prevent sticking)
  2. Add pasta and continue boiling for 10-15 minutes, depending on preferred consistency
  3. Dice onion, mushroom, capsicum and celery
  4. Heat up oil in a large frying pan or wok
  5. Saute onion, celery and capsicum
  6. Add frozen meatballs and carrot
  7. Cook for ten minutes, then add marinara sauce, cover and cook for 5 more minutes
  8. Drain the spaghetti and add to frying pan, mixing it through the sauce
  9. Grate vegan parmesan on top of each serving
