Chickpea Spread

Description:

This is a delicious sandwich filling. Similar to tuna or chicken salad, this healthy alternative is a must go-to for a quick and satisfying lunch. You can easily tweak this recipe to appease your taste buds!

Ingredients:

  • 1 tin chickpeas, rinsed
  • 1 stalk celery
  • 1/2 small onion
  • 2 medium-sized gherkins
  • 1/4 cup dried cranberries
  • 1/4 cup walnut pieces
  • 3 tablespoons aioli or mayo
  • 1 tablespoon mustard
  • salt and pepper (a pinch of each, to taste)

Steps:

  1. Blend chickpeas, walnuts, and cranberries to desired consistency
  2. Dice onion, gherkins and celery
  3. Add chickpea mixture, diced veggies, sauces and seasoning into a mixing bowl
  4. Mix everything together and add extra ingredients, to taste
  5. Serve on fresh bread or toast. Yum!
